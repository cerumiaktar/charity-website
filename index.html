FAQ

What is the mission of your charity?
Our mission is to support individuals and communities in need by providing resources, programs, and services that create lasting positive change.
How can I donate to your charity?
You can donate securely through our website using a credit/debit card or other available payment options. All donations go directly toward supporting our programs.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, donations are tax-deductible where allowed by law. After donating, you will receive a receipt for your records.
How are donations used?
Donations are used to fund our projects, support our beneficiaries, and cover essential operational costs to ensure our programs run effectively and sustainably.
How can I volunteer or get involved?
You can get involved by volunteering, fundraising, or spreading awareness. Visit our “Get Involved” page to learn about current opportunities and sign up.
